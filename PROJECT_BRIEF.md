# Open Masjid (Masj.id) - Ecosystem Brief

## 🌟 Vision
Digitalisasi pengelolaan masjid Indonesia yang Amanah, Transparan, dan Modern.

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. Finance & Transparency
- **Automated Bank Mutation:** Support BCA, BSI, Mandiri CSV import.
- **Detailed Public Reports:** Real-time financial transparency at `masj.id/username/laporan`.
- **Program-Based Funding:** Dedicated pockets for specific mosque projects.

### 2. Digital Engagement
- **TV Display Mode:** Professional digital signage for mosque monitors.
- **WhatsApp Integration:** Direct communication with jamaah and mustahik.
- **Public Profiles:** Personalized landing pages for every mosque.

### 3. Social Impact
- **Mustahik Database:** Economic profiling for accurate aid distribution.
- **Distribution Tracking:** Documenting aid delivery with evidence.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- CodeIgniter 4 (PHP)
- Tailwind CSS
- MySQL
- Integrated with Payment Gateways & AlAdhan API
